We need an internal leave request management system to replace our current
spreadsheet-based process. Employees should be able to submit leave requests
(annual leave, sick leave, parental leave) which their line managers approve
or reject. HR should have full visibility across the organisation — team
calendars, pending requests, approval history. The system needs to integrate
with our existing HRIS (BambooHR) and our payroll system. We want this live
within four months. The primary users are our 340 employees across three
offices (Warsaw, London, Berlin).
