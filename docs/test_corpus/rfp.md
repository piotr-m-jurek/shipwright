# Request for Proposal — Internal HR Systems Modernisation

**Reference:** HR-2024-07
**Issuing department:** Human Resources
**Response deadline:** 2024-12-01

---

## 1. Background

Meridian Group (340 employees, offices in Warsaw, London, and Berlin) is
seeking a vendor or internal development team to deliver a modernised leave
management system as part of a broader HR systems initiative.

---

## 2. Scope of work

The system must cover the full leave request lifecycle: submission, manager
approval, HR oversight, and payroll handoff. Full requirements are detailed
in the attached PRD draft.

---

## 3. Timeline

The solution must be delivered and in production within 16 weeks of project
kickoff. A phased delivery is acceptable provided core submission and approval
flows are live within 10 weeks.

---

## 4. Integration requirements

The system must integrate with BambooHR (employee data, reporting lines,
entitlements) via BambooHR's REST API. Payroll integration via CSV export
is acceptable for V1.

---

## 5. Authentication

The system should use the organisation's existing authentication mechanism.
SSO via Google Workspace is preferred but not mandatory for V1.

---

## 6. Support and maintenance

The delivering team must provide 3 months of post-launch support. SLA for
critical issues: 4-hour response, 24-hour resolution.

---

## 7. Data residency and compliance

All employee personal data processed and stored by this system must remain
within the European Union at all times. The system must comply with GDPR
requirements including data subject access rights, right to erasure, and
data processing agreements. Any cloud infrastructure used must be
EU-region only. This is a non-negotiable requirement given the personal
nature of the data processed (health information in sick leave records).

---

## 8. Evaluation criteria

Proposals will be evaluated on: technical approach (30%), timeline realism
(25%), team experience (25%), cost (20%).

---

## 9. Submission instructions

Proposals must be submitted to hr-systems@meridiangroup.com by 2024-12-01.
Include: technical approach, team CVs, timeline breakdown, and cost estimate.
